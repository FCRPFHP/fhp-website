The Florida Highway Patrol is the finest state law enforcement agency in the nation and we provide the highest level of professional law enforcement services available. We hold both National and State law enforcement accreditation. Using a comprehensive education and enforcement plan we promote safety throughout Florida.

We have many exciting career and promotional opportunities. Troopers who meet time in grade requirement are eligible for specialty positions such as contraband interdiction officer, criminal investigations, instructor, pilot, public affairs officer, special response team, commercial vehicle enforcement, K-9 handler, plus many more including the following:

• Quick Response Force
• Bureau of Criminal Investigations and Intelligence
• Academy/Recruitment/Background Investigations
• Commercial Vehicle Enforcement
• Drug Recognition Expert
• Motorcycles
• Contraband Interdiction/K-9
• Special Response Team (SWAT)
• Hazardous Material Squad
• Compliance Investigations

Promotion Opportunities: Investigator, Corporal, Sergeant, Lieutenant, Captain

State of the Art Equipment:
• Computers, printers, and fingerprint scanners in cars.
• Revolutionary emergency lighting packages.
• Pursuit and specialty vehicles.
• In-car, 360-degree video cameras.
• Uniforms and weapons
• And much, much more!
