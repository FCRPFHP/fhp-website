Special Response Team

A Special Response Team AKA (SRT) team is a designated law enforcement team, whose members are recruited, selected, trained, equipped and assigned to resolve critical incidents involving a threat to public safety which would otherwise exceed the capabilities of traditional law enforcement first responders.

The mission of the Florida Highway Patrol Special Response Team is to deploy a statewide multitude of emergency service elements when called upon. The team operates in specialized environments such as land, sea, and air, which includes their subdivisions as well; Hostage Negotiation Unit, Marine/Dive Unit, Air Assault Team, and the Personnel Protection Unit. All members of the highway patrol Special Weapons Team are cross-trained between all four subdivisions to ensure maximum efficiency and capability at all times.
