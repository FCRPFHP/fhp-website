To be eligible to apply for employment you must have one of the following:

• IRL Active, Reserve or Retired Military Service

• IRL Certified Law Enforcement/Corrections

• IRL Employment with Law Enforcement Agency in Civilian Capacity

• Certificate in Public Safety, Criminal Justice/Law

• 30 days of service with a law enforcement agency in Florida Coast

• 30 days of Service as a Florida Highway Patrol Auxiliary Trooper

Yes. We only open our applications as needed, making the hiring process very competitive.

Step 1:

Complete an online application: Go to our apply now page and join our discord.

Step 2

FHP recruiters will review all applications. Applicants should make sure they meet all requirements prior to applying and submitted applications have substantial detail.

Step 3

Once an application passes step 2, a background will be completed on the applicant.

Step 4

After the application is accepted and the applicant passes the background investigation, the applicant will schedule an interview with an FHP recruiter.

Step 5

If an applicant successfully completes their interview, they will be processed into the pre-academy preparation. This includes taken a written exam.

Step 6

Once the applicant completes their written exam, it will be sent to the FHP review board for final determination. If the applicant successfully passes our review board, an offer of employment will be given, and the applicant will transfer.

Step 7

Applicants are offered employment and attend the Florida Highway Patrol Training Academy!
