Traffic Homicide Investigations

The Florida Highway Patrol Traffic Homicide Investigation (FHP THI) unit is dedicated to meticulously investigating traffic crashes resulting in fatalities. Our mission is to unravel the complexities of these incidents, determine causative factors, and seek justice for victims and their families. Through expert accident reconstruction, forensic analysis, and collaborative efforts with law enforcement agencies and legal authorities, we strive to ensure accountability and promote safety on Florida's roadways.

The operation of the Florida Highway Patrol Traffic Homicide Investigation unit encompasses a comprehensive approach to investigating traffic crashes resulting in fatalities. It begins with a rapid and efficient response to crash scenes, where the primary objective is to secure the area promptly while initiating the necessary investigative protocols. A critical aspect of this operation involves meticulous evidence collection and preservation, ensuring that all pertinent physical evidence, including vehicle debris, skid marks, and witness statements, is carefully documented and safeguarded for further analysis and legal proceedings. The unit employs advanced techniques in accident reconstruction to unravel the complex sequence of events leading up to the crash, meticulously examining factors such as vehicle dynamics, speeds, and environmental conditions to determine causative factors. Additionally, forensic analysis plays a crucial role in the investigation, with skilled investigators conducting thorough examinations of vehicles and drivers to identify potential mechanical failures, impairment, or other contributing elements.

Moreover, the unit places a strong emphasis on gathering eyewitness testimonies, recognizing their invaluable insights into the circumstances surrounding the crash. Collaboration is key within the unit, as well as with other law enforcement agencies, medical examiners, and legal authorities, facilitating the exchange of information and expertise to ensure a comprehensive investigation. Finally, the unit provides vital legal support by offering expert testimony and evidence in legal proceedings, supporting prosecution efforts, and advocating for justice on behalf of the victims and their families.
